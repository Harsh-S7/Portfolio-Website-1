My raison d'etre? Weltanschauung?

Obsessively curious about bettering my craft. And myself.

I come from a tiny village in Goa, but the Internet has been a great equalizer. My curiosity for design and computers took root early. Since 2009, I’ve been learning from books, blogs, podcasts and other designers. It never ends.

My life’s goal is to craft products with care, consistency and passion. I was fortunate to have my early work featured in The Web Designer’s Idea Book Vol. 2 & Vol. 3, Web Design Index No. 9 and Smashing Magazine. Now that’s a start!

Morals and ethics are my north star. I will always champion for product and business decisions based on the greater good of users.

I believe in asking for forgiveness, not permission. In keeping my work legible. In having the freedom to make mistakes. In balance in every area of my life. And a bunch of other things.

I’ve been working remotely since mid-2018, and it has literally changed my life. True story.

Outside work, I enjoy spending time with my family. I also read, write and take photos. I keep fit with a mix of strength training and beach soccer.

I live and work in the city of Panaji. Someday, I’ll retire to a quiet cabin by the beach.
